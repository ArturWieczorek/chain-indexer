# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a friendly and harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.
This is a teaching project; questions from beginners are welcome and expected.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that helps:

- Being respectful, patient, and welcoming.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the people learning from it.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer at
**artur.wieczorek@iohk.io**. Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
The maintainer may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that do not
align with this code of conduct, and may ban repeat offenders.
